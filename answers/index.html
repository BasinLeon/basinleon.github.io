What is revenue architecture?

Revenue architecture is the system design layer behind pipeline outcomes: signal capture, routing logic, handoff governance, and conversion instrumentation.

How is this different from traditional RevOps?

Traditional RevOps often reports on funnel performance after breakdowns. Revenue architecture rebuilds the execution logic underneath the funnel to reduce leakage before it happens.

What measurable outcomes can this approach produce?

Published outcomes include $40M+ pipeline impact, 160% YoY growth in one enterprise context, and $424K annual Opex savings from routing and workflow redesign.

Who should use a signal-first GTM system?

Founder-led and technical B2B teams with noisy lead flow, unclear ownership, weak stage conversion, or high manual burden should prioritize signal-first routing architecture.
