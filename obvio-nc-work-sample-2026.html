Qualification + handoff

A qualified meeting is my definition, not the prospect's.

Qualified — all three present
✓They name a specific corridor, school zone, or intersection
✓There's a real internal path: Chief, Manager, or council access
✓They agree to a follow-up meeting to review pilot logic
Disqualify early if
✗Pain is vague with no urgency
✗No one can name who else needs to be involved
✗Purely informational — no willingness for a concrete next step
