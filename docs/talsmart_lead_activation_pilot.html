How It Works

Three clean steps for a showable, sellable first engagement.

This is the simplest version Jassi can bring into partner and client conversations without overcomplicating the offer.

01

Define the target corridor

02

Pilot Model

Small enough to buy. Clear enough to measure.

The first engagement should be framed as a fixed pilot with obvious boundaries. That keeps the sale easy, the delivery focused, and the result easier to compare.

Starting scope One market, one offer line, or one team rather than a full business transformation.
Client inputs ICP, offer list, target accounts, current baseline, and messaging constraints.
Measurement Lead fit, speed to response, meetings sourced, and downstream progression.
Decision rule Scale only if the pilot proves out against the current benchmark.
