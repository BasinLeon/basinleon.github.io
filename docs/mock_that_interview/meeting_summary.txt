Mock That Interview
Tue, Mar 10, 2026

Summary:

The meeting reviewed Mock That Interview’s product, go-to-market position, and user acquisition metrics to solicit GTM and growth advice. After participant introductions, the founders described the platform as a hybrid B2C/B2B SaaS offering with interview simulation, job search, AI-generated cover letters and CVs, and application tracking; they reported about 200 registered users (mostly technical profiles) and a prospect list of roughly 50+ bootcamp leads stored in Excel. Most traffic has been organic SEO rather than paid, analytics and churn-tracking are limited, and one enterprise inbound lead is evaluating an internal build versus the platform.

Discussion then covered how prospects access the product via the website and booking flow and the current trial model that issues limited free credits; the team agreed to reduce the free-credit trial from 10 to 3. Advisors recommended concentrating outbound efforts on universities and bootcamps, running A/B tests on messaging and channels, and increasing inbound tactics such as SEO and webinars. The team committed to producing a 30/60/90 GTM plan and improving measurement of user behavior and monetization to convert free users into paying customers.
