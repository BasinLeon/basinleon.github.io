# Addendum to Professional Services Agreement

Effective Date: April 17, 2026  
Parties: Basin::Nexus (Contractor) and Mock That Interview (Client)

## 1. Retainer Adjustments

The monthly retainer for Fractional GTM and Revenue Architecture services shall scale based on performance milestones.

### 1.1 Phase 1

Client shall pay Contractor $500 per month until the Platform reaches 500 active users.

### 1.2 Phase 2

Upon reaching 500 active users and establishing a consistent institutional pipeline, the monthly retainer shall increase to $1,000.

### 1.3 Phase 3

Upon reaching 1,000 active users and providing proof of placement or institutional revenue, the monthly retainer shall increase to $1,500.

## 2. Performance Incentives (Poaching Engine)

### 2.1 Commission

Client shall pay Contractor a commission of 15% on all revenue generated through the Poaching Engine infrastructure.

### 2.2 Definition

Poaching Engine revenue includes institutional pilot fees, enterprise licenses, and placement fees derived from intercepted candidates.

### 2.3 Payment

Commissions are due within 15 days of Client receiving payment from the respective third party.
