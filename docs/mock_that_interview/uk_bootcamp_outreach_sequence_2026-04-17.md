# UK Bootcamp Outreach Sequence

Date: 2026-04-17  
Segment: UK bootcamp career outcomes teams  
Goal: Book qualified conversations about placement readiness infrastructure

## Sequence Principles

- Lead with outcomes pressure, not product feature detail
- Keep language operator-level and specific
- Ask for a short baseline review (15-20 min)
- Avoid inflated claims that cannot be evidenced live
