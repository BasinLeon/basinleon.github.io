## The Logic Gap: The Bidding Bottleneck
In the general labor and construction sector (windows, installation, electrical), winning business is a numbers game that most companies find impossible to play. 

*   **The Commodity Problem:** Estimators spend 80% of their time manually reading proposals, sorting through portals (BuildingConnected), and rewriting bids.
*   **The Yield Loss:** Because the process is manual, companies can only bid on a fraction of available opportunities, leading to "Revenue Leakage" and slow growth.

## The Nexus Solution: Ether AI
Ether AI moves the bidding process from a manual labor task to an **Automated Yield Engine**.

1.  **Portal Extraction:** 80% automation of lead extraction from fragmented bidding sites (BuildingConnected, etc.).
2.  **The Bid-Shield:** Automated sorting and prioritization of jobs based on "Win Probability" rather than first-come-first-served.
3.  **Revenue Velocity:** By reducing the bid-time by $4\times$, Ether AI allows a lean team to bid on the entire market volume, not just the low-hanging fruit.

## The Partnership Architecture (The Split)
This model relies on a clean, structural separation between high-frequency signal generation (the tech) and real-world delivery (the labor).

*   **The AI/Technology Hub (Leon & Garrett):** The builders of the "Ether AI" infrastructure. We eliminate the manual data entry, automate the follow-ups, ingest the BuildingConnected PDFs, and provide the overarching Business/GTM strategy. We turn the bidding process into software.
*   **The Physical Execution Hub (Murad & Armen):** The domain experts who own the physical reality. They actually build the windows, do the painting, run the electrical, and manage the general labor crews.
