### 1. The Logic Gap Analysis
Most EdTech platforms stop at the **Signal** (providing a practice tool). The **Yield** (the actual job offer) is left to chance. 
`MockThatInterview` has the technology to bridge this gap. By combining AI behavioral simulation with Human expert calibration, we move from "Practice" to **"Placement Readiness."**

### 2. The S2Y (Signal-to-Yield) Framework for MTI
We will architect the platform as a **Placement Infrastructure** layer for B2B partners.
*   **Signal**: User uploads CV & Job Description.
*   **Refinery**: AI + Human evaluation creates a "Hire-Readiness" score.
*   **Yield**: A direct line to placement partners (Bootcamps, Hiring firms) with verified candidate performance data.
