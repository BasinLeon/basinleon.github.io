Core model / GTM interpretation

Signal → intent → route

Do not route raw activity. Capture the signal, interpret what it means now, then assign a path and owner.

  1. Capture role changes, engagement, referrals, and problem language.
  2. Classify stage, urgency, confidence, and likely motion.
  3. Route to nurture, conversation, partner, not now, or urgent.
