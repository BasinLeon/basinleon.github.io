Revenue Activation Layer for digital pipeline flow

This is the small, showable continuation layer Jassi described: NBC keeps doing what it is already doing, while Leon's layer brings in additional qualified digital opportunities, refines them, and hands them to the current sales team.

Once NBC's Account Executives are better prepared to sell digital solutions, the next challenge is not only readiness. It is flow. Teams can complete training and still lose momentum when stronger digital opportunities do not reach the right seller quickly enough or arrive without enough fit to justify fast follow-up. The Revenue Activation Layer is designed to operate alongside NBC's current sales motion as an additional source of qualified digital opportunities, without requiring a rebuild of the team's existing systems.
What This Layer Does
  • Identify target digital buyers against NBC's agreed ICP, market priorities, and approved opportunity criteria.
  • Refine and route those opportunities into the current sales motion so the right AE or market owner can engage while intent is still live.
  • Compare lead quality, response timing, and downstream progression against the current baseline before expanding scope.
Why This Is Easy To Buy
  • No CRM replacement. NBC keeps its current system.
  • No seller disruption. NBC's current team still owns follow-up and closing.
  • Los Angeles first. One market, one pilot, one comparison logic.

The recommended starting point is a focused Los Angeles pilot. NBC keeps its current CRM, sellers, and operating rhythm. The pilot adds an external lead-generation and refinement layer, creates a weekly leadership review cadence, and gives NBC a low-risk way to evaluate whether the model should scale across additional teams or markets.
