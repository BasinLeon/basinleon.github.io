TalSmart Reply Playbook

From: Leon Basin  |  Date: March 10, 2026  |  For: Jassi, Rohit, and the outreach team

How to use this

  1. Read the prospect's reply.
  2. Find the matching signal type below.
  3. Copy the template, fill in the brackets.
  4. If they asked for a meeting, skip the template and book the call directly.

Team rules

  • If they ask for a call, book the call. Do not send a form instead.
  • One thing to do per message. Not two.
  • Keep it professional. No "Ha Ha," no LOL, no jokes.
  • VP+ or Big 4 background? Flag to Leon or Jassi before replying.
  • Video channel is the last touch on a dead thread. Use it once.
  • The 4th-Touch Rule: If a contact says 'No' after you've already sent 4+ messages in the cadence, do not try the 'wrong person' redirect. Immediately use the Warm Exit (video channel subscription) to preserve the relationship without adding noise.
  • The Udemy course is never a "discount." It is a VIP audit pass to review the methodology. Frame it that way every time.
  • Pro tip: when ending any reply, you can add: "Curious if this is even relevant for your team right now, or if I'm completely off base?" This lowers resistance and invites a response even from people who would otherwise ignore the message.
  • The Awareness Protocol: For cold outreach (LinkedIn Signal), refer to the Neural Outreach Architecture. Choose the 'Curiosity' hook based on the persona's Awareness Level (Unaware, Problem Aware, or Solution Aware).
