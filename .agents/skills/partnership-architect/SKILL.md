# Partnership Architect

## Overview

Transform "vendor" conversations into "architectural" alliances. Maintain a high-status, operater-first tone that signals you are a fellow builder, not a salesman.

## When to Use

- Initiating outreach to founders/execs after a manual hiring or scale trigger.
- Replying to partnership offers (credits, affiliate links, referral fees).
- Designing "Commercial Mechanics" (revenue share, fractional support, agency onboarding).
- **Red Flags**: If you find yourself using numbered lists, generic openers ("Following up...", "I'm excited to..."), or "salesy" language.

## Core Principles

### 1. The Operator Dialect

Avoid abstract nouns. Use concrete, system-level terminology.

| Avoid (Consultant) | Use (Operator) |
| :--- | :--- |
| "Help you grow" | "Scale the distribution" |
| "Improve efficiency" | "Optimize the routing/execution behavior" |
| "Interested in partnership" | "Design the commercial mechanics" |
| "See how it works" | "Vet the demand side behavior" |
| "Happy to collaborate" | "Map out the architecture" |

### 2. The Conductor Play

Position yourself (Leon) as the **Logic Layer** (The Conductor) and the partner as the **Execution Layer** (The Muscle).

- **Partner (e.g., Bounty)**: Provides the "Compute" or "Manual Labor" (bounties, tasks).
- **Basin::Nexus**: Provides the "Brain" (Signal Refinery, GTM architecture).
- **Goal**: Don't just refer users; build blueprints that *require* the partner's tool.

### 3. No "Consultant Theater"

- **Short & Direct**: One idea per paragraph. Max 3-4 paragraphs.
- **Executive Close**: "I'll book time shortly" vs "Let me know what works for you."
- **NEXUS Triggers**: Reference THE_VACUUM, THE_PIVOT, or THE_AGENTIC_PLAY if appropriate for the internal logic.

## Examples

### Good (Smart Operator)

> David—
> 
> Noticed the push for 10 new SDRs. 
> 
> Most scaling teams solve top-of-funnel by adding headcount. We’re taking a different approach with Basin::Nexus. 
> 
> We’ve built the 'Signal Refinery' — an automated outreach layer that handles the signal-to-execution loop before it hits a human. It’s about decoupling volume from headcount growth.
> 
> I’d like to show you the architecture. If it makes sense, we can plug it into your existing stack and let your SDRs focus on finishing, not starting.
> 
> I'll book time shortly.
> 
> Leon

### Bad (Consultant Heavy)

> Dear David, 
> 
> I hope this email finds you well! I saw that Sentry is hiring for 10 new SDRs and I'm very excited to discuss how we can help. 
> 
> At Basin & Associates, we specialize in:
> 1. GTM Strategy
> 2. Automated Outreach
> 3. Efficiency Audits
> 
> I'd love to jump on a 30-minute call to dive into your current processes and see where we can collaborate. Let me know when you're free!
> 
> Best,
> Leon

| "They offered credits" | Credits are for users. Architects get revenue share. |
