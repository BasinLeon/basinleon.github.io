# Diagnostic-Led Outreach

## Use when
Use this skill when you have a target lead (from **Signal Scout Pro**) and need to "Break the Pattern." This skill generates "Lures" that force a conversation about Architecture rather than Pricing.

## The Diagnostic "Lures"
Generate outreach copy that focuses on these three diagnostic pillars:
1.  **The Signal Leak**: Pointing out an obvious system failure (e.g., "I noticed your team is posting 5 JDs for SDRs but zero for GTM Engineering. You're scaling a bottleneck.")
2.  **The Revenue Transition**: Identifying a structural shift (e.g., "Moving from Linear to Digital isn't a training problem; it's a systems motion problem.")
3.  **The Speculative Audit**: Offering a "Mini-audit" of their current perception (e.g., "I've mapped how the market views your current digital inventory. Want to see the gap?")

## High-Status Mechanics
- **Lead with Authority**: Never ask "if" they have a problem. State exactly where you suspect the problem lives based on their public signals.
- **Low-Friction Inquiries**: Instead of "Can we chat?", use "Interested in the data?" or "Should I ship the brief?"
- **The "Bypass" Move**: Copy should be written to be forwardable from a Gatekeeper (Kevin) to a Driver (Jeff Wynne).

## Example Template
"Hi {{NAME}}, 
Most sales groups in the {{INDUSTRY}} transition are losing 30-40% of their digital yield simply because their 'Linear' discovery process doesn't match the new digital buyer motion. 

I've put together a 1-page **Digital Revenue Diagnostic** for the {{TEAM_NAME}} team that outlines where these mechanical leaks usually happen. 

Should I send it over for your leadership team to review before your Q2 meeting?"
