# Obvio Territory Intel

## Purpose
Turn territory research into actionable private GTM memory: what to pursue, why now, who matters, what risk exists, and what the next clean action should be.

## Default Output

```markdown
## Territory / Account

### Current Read

### Decision Logic

### Stakeholders

### Timing Window

### Risks / Constraints

### Next Action

### CRM Hygiene
```

## Research Discipline
- Separate verified facts from inference.
- Preserve source links when available.
- Mark stale, unverified, or risky data clearly.
- Identify blockers, incumbent risk, policy risk, and process uncertainty.
- Convert research into a next action, not a static dossier.

## CRM Readiness
When asked to prepare CRM import or cleanup guidance, identify:

- Company / municipality record
- Contact records
- Deal or opportunity records
- Required fields
- Optional intelligence fields
- Ownership and next task

Do not expose CRM schemas publicly.

## Security Boundary
This skill is private-only by default. Territory names, stakeholder names, timing windows, city lists, deal strategy, and outreach sequence details belong in Obsidian or local private files.

If the user asks to publish any derivative, route through `obvio-public-safe-publisher`.

## Storage
Prefer the private operating-ledger folder:

`Leon's Journal/01_PROJECTS/Obvio_GTM_Operating_Ledger/`

For reusable patterns that contain no private facts, a generic skill or public-safe note may be created separately.
