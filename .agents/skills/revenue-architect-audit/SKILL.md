# Revenue Architect Audit

## Use when
Use this skill for any Tier-1 prospect (like NBC or Altruist). This is a "Heavy Research" skill that generates a document for the "Champion" to show their boss.

## The Audit Protocol
1.  **The Desktop Scan**: Analyze their public stack, hiring history, and product updates.
2.  **The Friction Hypothesis**: Where is their revenue leaking?
    - *Common:* High SDR-to-AE ratio (+Manual Grind).
    - *Common:* Fragmented Digital/Linear teams.
    - *Common:* No automated feedback loop between sales and product.
3.  **The "Speculative" Solution**: A high-status draft of what their **Optimized Revenue OS** would look like.

## Output Structure
- **The Gap Analysis**: Identifying the distance between where they are and the "4% Elite."
- **The Yield Forecast**: Estimated ROI of fixing the architecture.
- **The Founder Track**: How Leon’s background directly solves their specific friction point.

## High-Status Principles
- **Lead with the Problem**: The audit shouldn't say "You're doing great." It should say "You're ignoring a structural failure."
- **Don't Be a Vendor**: The tone is "Peer Auditor" or "Board Advisor."
- **The "Agentic" Angle**: Always include how AI/Agents can solve their specific manual bottleneck.
