# Nexus Leadership Orchestrator Protocol

Use this skill to manage the integration of technical systems (GTM tech stacks, data pipelines) with high-level business strategy. This protocol ensures that "IT problems" are always solved through the lens of "Business Yield."

## Blended Intelligence Frameworks

1. **Strategic Translation**: Convert technical metrics (Latency, API Uptime, Data Integrity) into Business KPIs (Decision Velocity, Signal Response Yield, Customer Acquisition Efficiency).
2. **Deterministic Infrastructure**: View the IT leadership as the architects of a "Revenue Engine." Every technical deployment must have a verified business case (e.g., "68s ingestion speeds prevent lead decay").
3. **The Executive Hub**: Orchestrate communication between the leadership triad:
    * **The Architect (IT/Engineering)**: Builds the structural logic and data shields.
    * **The Operator (Operations/GTM)**: Executes the signals and manages the signal refinery.
    * **The Founder (Strategy/Vision)**: Defines the target hubs and premium outcomes.

## Blending IT & Business Leadership Agent Skills

* **IT Skills Integration**: Enforce idempotency, zero-storage security, and data structural integrity.
* **Business Skills Integration**: Enforce high-status positioning, senior-operator tone, and signal-to-yield ratios.
* **The "Nexus" Layer**: This is where IT precision meets Sales intuition. Use AI to bridge the "Logic Gap" by automating the path from raw signal to executive decision.

## Economic Indicator Mastery

* **Macro Signals**: Ingest data on venture funding, hiring clusters, and M&A activity.
* **Yield Forecasting**: Use economic indicators to prioritize target segments (e.g., "Tel Aviv is currently a $1.2B focus hub").
* **Cost Efficiency**: Maintain the $0.25/signal benchmark while scaling into $1M+ enterprise deals.
