# External Lead Pilot Page Wireframe

Use this wireframe for a simple page that a partner can show and the team can later ship as a live lead-capture asset.

## Section 1: Hero

- Headline: one promise
- Subhead: who it is for and what stays intact
- CTA: one button only

Example:

```md
# Add a Qualified Lead Layer Without Rebuilding Your Sales System

External GTM execution for teams that want more high-fit opportunities routed into the motion they already run.

[Request Pilot Walkthrough]
```

## Section 2: No-Disruption Strip

Use 3 short proof lines:

- No CRM replacement
- No added SDR headcount required to start
- Pilot-first with baseline comparison

## Section 3: Three-Step Flow

```md
1. Identify and qualify the right accounts
2. Refine and activate against your ICP
3. Hand qualified opportunities to your current team
```

## Section 4: Proof / Fit

Keep this short:

- who it is for
- what use case it fits
- one result, benchmark, or credibility signal

## Section 5: Form or Booking CTA

Default fields:

- full name
- work email
- company
- role
- biggest pipeline or lead bottleneck

If traffic is low-intent, use a booking CTA instead of a long form.

## Copy guardrails

- one CTA above the fold
- no equal-weight offer tiles
- no long methodology dump
- no giant diagnostic before email capture
- every section should move the reader forward
