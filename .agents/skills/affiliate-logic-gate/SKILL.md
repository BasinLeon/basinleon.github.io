# [SKILL] Affiliate Logic Gate
**Objective**: Filter for high-yield, high-status affiliate and partnership programs.

## ⚖️ The Filter Criteria
The Swarm only accepts programs that meet the **"Nexus-3"** criteria:
1.  **Strategic Depth**: The product must solve a "Logic Gap" in the GTM stack (e.g., Clay, n8n, Census).
2.  **Yield Efficiency**: High commission (20%+) or performance-based payout (like Naano's 1€/click) that scales with engagement.
3.  **Brand Alignment**: The brand must be "Operator-Grade." No commodity spam tools.

## 🛠️ The Affirmation Loop
Before recommending a sign-up, the agent must verify:
*   **Cookie Duration**: Minimum 30 days (Reward for long-tail authority).
*   **Payout Frequency**: Monthly or engagement-triggered.
*   **Asset Quality**: Does the brand provide clean hooks and "Problem-Led" assets?
