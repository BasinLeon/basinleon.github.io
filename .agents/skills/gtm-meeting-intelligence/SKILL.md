# GTM Meeting Intelligence

## Use when
Use this skill 24 hours before any strategic call. It takes the "Diagnostic Brief" and turns it into a "Conversation Control Map."

## The Battle Card Protocol
Every Battle Card must include:
1.  **The Mirror**: 3 things the prospect said (or their JD says) that we will repeat back to them to show "Deep Discovery."
2.  **The Tipping Point**: The specific question that makes them realize their current process is broken.
3.  **Narrative Anchors**: 3 phrases to repeat (e.g., "Revenue OS," "Signal Layer," "Manual Grind").
4.  **Objection Neutralizers**: Handling "We have a trainer," "It's too expensive," or "We'll do this in Q4."
5.  **The Technical Proof**: A specific automation or logic flow (e.g., "Signal Refinery") to show as a "Proprietary Asset."

## High-Status Principles
- **Talk Architecture, Not Tasks**: Never explain "how" you'll do something; explain the **Logic** you'll deploy.
- **Lower the Verbal Velocity**: Instructions for the speaker to speak slower and use pauses to command the room.
- **The "Takeaway" Move**: If they seem hesitant, have a script for politely "taking the deal away" to test their seriousness.
