# LB CRM People Orbit Sync

Use this skill when updating Leon's relationship map.

## Objective
Keep the people layer synced with the opportunity layer so warm intros, recruiters, founders, and partners do not drift out of context.

## Core Fields

- name
- company
- relationship
- current status
- next step
- linked opportunity or lane

## Workflow

### 1. Extract the people list
Pull named contacts from:

- People in Orbit sections
- opportunity cards
- intro emails
- meeting notes

### 2. Link person to lane
Every person should connect to one of:

- job search
- revenue opportunity
- partner lane
- networking
- speaking / academic

### 3. Preserve relationship status
Examples:

- recruiter
- co-founder
- partner
- warm intro
- academic
- technical partner

### 4. Write the next step
Do not leave people as static names.
Capture the motion.

## Principle

A CRM without relationship motion is just a graveyard of names.
