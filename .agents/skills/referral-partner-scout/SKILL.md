# Basin::Nexus Referral & Partner Scout Protocol (v1.0)

## Overview
The `referral-partner-scout` skill initializes and manages the economic lifecycle of strategic referrals. It ensures high-status alignment with network partners (Scouts) by leveraging a "Tiered Yield Override" rather than a flat, commodity referral fee.

## The "Scout" Economic Framework
| Phase | Action | Yield to Scout | Objective |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **01: Intro Strike** | Qualified referral provided. | **$200 Fixed** | Secure the network "Lead Flow" priority. |
| **02: Retainer Lock** | Retainer is paid by client. | $0 (Covered in intro fee) | Neutralize operator risk. |
| **03: Yield Override** | Commission is earned by Leon. | **10-15% of Leon's Share** | Incentivize lead quality and longevity. |

## Operational Lifecycle

### 1. The Engagement "Strike"
When a scout provides a referral, initialize the **"Partner Lead Score"**:
- **Identity**: Who is the Scout? (e.g., Ola)
- **Signal**: What is the client's problem? (e.g., MTI needs a poaching engine)
- **Yield Potential**: What is the floor ($500) and the upside (15% commission)?

### 2. Settlement Protocol
- **Intro Fee**: Paid upon the **first** retainer clearance.
- **Override Settlement**: Calculated and paid within 7 days of Leon receiving the **Performance Commission.**

### 3. Communication Pattern
- **Status Sync**: "Ridwan is moving. I've staged your $200 intro fee for the first retainer hit. Let's build."
- **Proof of Yield**: "We just hit the MTI Day 90 KPI. Your performance override is staged."
