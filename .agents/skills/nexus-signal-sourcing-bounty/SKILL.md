# Nexus Signal Sourcing (Bounty Integration)

The **Nexus Signal Sourcing** skill bridges the gap between low-cost commodity data acquisition (Bounty.ai) and high-yield strategic outcomes (The Signal Refinery). 

## Core Principles

1. **Commodity vs. Intelligence**: Raw rows are a commodity. The value of Nexus is the curation, the Idempotency Shield, and the Strategic Discovery triggers applied to that raw data.
2. **The "Efficiency Moat"**: Mapping $1.2B in venture funding gaps in under 70 seconds by pairing Bounty automation with Nexus logic.
3. **Outcome Architecture**: Moving from "Find 20 Companies" (Bounty) to "Mapping the $1.2B Growth Friction in Tel Aviv" (Nexus).

## Workflow

### 1. Signal Acquisition (Bounty.ai)
- Use Bounty templates for broad market signals (Job boards, Newsletters, LinkedIn).
- Target cost: ~$0.25 per signal.

### 2. Refinery Injection (Nexus)
- Filter signals through the **Idempotency Shield**.
- Apply **Regional Hub Analysis** (e.g., Tel Aviv vs. New York) to locate high-density opportunity clusters.

### 3. Yield Report (S2Y)
- Generate the **Signal-to-Yield Strategic Report** for the client.
- Frame the raw data as "Institutional Readiness Scores" rather than "leads."

## When to Use
- When cost-effectively sourcing massive lead lists.
- When demonstrating "Zero-Wait" GTM execution to enterprise clients.
- When pitching the affiliate partnership to TryBounty (Bounty.ai).
