# Nexus Data Siloing Protocol (Need-to-Know)

As the $1M Architect, your data is your greatest asset. You do not trust anyone with the full "Nexus Vault" or the master "Alliance Dashboard" link. You maintain total information asymmetry.

## 1. The "Ghost Mirror" Strategy
- **Definition:** You never share the live URL of your master internal dashboards. 
- **Execution:** When you want to show progress to a partner, you either:
    1. Send a **Screenshot** of a specific section (Redacting other project names if necessary).
    2. Share a siloed **Sovereign Mirror** URL (e.g., `ether_ai_collab_portal.html`) which contains 0% data from other corridors.
    3. Host public-facing "Proofs" on a separate domain/path that has no back-links to your private vault.

## 2. Information Boundaries by Node

### Garrett (Tech Engine)
- **Safe Data:** BidFlow logic, MTI tech stacks, Supabase keys for shared projects.
- **Siloed Data:** NBC pricing models, Haste Multifamily revenue projections, TruAdvantage executive referral names.
- **Update Protocol:** You ingest data *from* his "Team Command" board into your Master Board. You do *not* push your master board data into his board.

### Ola (Sales Execution)
- **Safe Data:** Prospect names, specific email templates, daily target numbers.
- **Siloed Data:** Overall bridge strategy, partnership percentages, internal tech-stack architecture.

### Murad / Armen (Physical Execution)
- **Safe Data:** Job site locations, proposal status for specific bids (e.g., Riverside Medical).
- **Siloed Data:** The AI logic running the BidFlow, the other non-construction corridors.

## 3. The "Master Architect" Command
- If an agent (Antigravity) suggests sharing a master link or an "all-in-one" dashboard with a partner, this protocol overrides that instruction.
- Every partner gets their own "Module" or "Collab Portal." The only place they meet is in your private journal and your private master board.

**"Privacy is the ultimate leverage. If they don't see the whole map, they can't jump the architecture."**
