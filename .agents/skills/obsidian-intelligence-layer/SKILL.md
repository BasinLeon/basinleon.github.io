# Obsidian Intelligence Layer

## When to Use
Use this skill when the user asks to integrate their current thinking in Obsidian with the AI, wants to draft content that follows the Basin::Nexus "Codex Hub" standards, or needs a thought partner to analyze vault research.

## The Process

### 1. Vault Sync & Context Loading
- **Identify Target**: Locate the relevant directory within `/Users/basin/Desktop/Basin & Associates 🌍/Leon's Journal/`.
- **Index Research**: Read the related `.md` files to build a current mental model of the user's research or drafts.
- **Reference Codex**: Check the `01_Codex Hub/01_Codex Hub.md` index to find related session logs and linked notes.

### 2. Intelligence Synthesis
- **Pattern Detection**: Look for recurring themes, structural contradictions, or revenue opportunities across multiple notes.
- **High-Status Reframing**: Elevate tactical notes into strategic frameworks. 
- **The "Thought Partner" Loop**: Instead of just summarizing, provide "Speculative Audits" or "Strategic Questions" back into the draft.

### 3. Collaborative Writing (Newsletters/Business/Academic)
- **Drafting**: Create new files or update existing ones in `/Users/basin/Desktop/Basin & Associates 🌍/Leon's Journal/02_Reference/` or relevant output folders.
- **Voice Calibration**: Ensure the writing sounds like the "Revenue Architect"—direct, senior, and outcome-focused.
- **Formatting**: Maintain Obsidian-friendly Markdown (wikilinks, clean headers, no AI fluff).

### 4. Closing the Loop
- **Logging**: Ensure every major update triggers a session log entry via the `log-session-to-obsidian` workflow.
- **Linking**: Automatically link new intelligence back to relevant master files (e.g., `LinkedIn Master CRM`, `Opportunities`, `Career Dashboard`).

## High-Status Principles
- **Don't just write; Architect**: Your goal is to organize the user's "Second Brain" into an operational weapon.
- **Avoid "AIisms"**: No "delve," "unlock," or "comprehensive landscapes." Use the user's natural operator voice.
- **Think structurally**: If the user is writing a newsletter, think about how it feeds the "Signal Refinery."

## Examples

### Good (Intelligence Layer)
"I've synthesized your last 3 research notes on 'Non-Linear Sales'. I noticed a gap in the 'Discovery' phase. I've drafted a new section in your 'Newsletter Draft' that addresses this specifically for VP Sales personas."

### Bad (Generic Writer)
"I have summarized your notes. Here is a newsletter about sales. It is very detailed and explores many things."
