# Nexus Bidding Automation (Ether AI)

The **Nexus Bidding Automation** skill focuses on the "Revenue Architecture" of physical labor and construction businesses. It uses the **Ether AI** logic to bridge the gap between fragmented bidding portals and final business wins.

## Execution Framework

### 1. Bidding Portal Intelligence
- Extraction of high-intent signals from portals like **BuildingConnected**.
- Moving from "Manual Data Entry" to **80% Automated Ingestion**.

### 2. The "Bid-to-Yield" (B2Y) Model
- **Volume over Friction**: Enabling a single operator to bid on $4\times$ the volume of a traditional estimator.
- **Hub Analysis**: Focusing bidding efforts on regional clusters (e.g., Bay Area) to optimize logistics and win rates.

### 3. Narrative Positioning
- Position automation as an "Earnings Advantage" for contractors.
- Frame the tech as a way to "Earn Business" without increasing staff overhead.

## Deployment Strategy
- Use for construction, installation (windows/doors), and trade-based leads.
- Deploy the "Zero-Wait" demo model: specifically automating the flow from the portal into the user's email inbox with pre-sorted lead scores.

## Key Terminology
- **BuildingConnected**: The primary commodity channel.
- **Ether AI**: The Nexus Logic Layer for bidding.
- **B2Y**: Bid-to-Yield.
