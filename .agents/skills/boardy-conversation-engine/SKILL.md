# Boardy Conversation Engine

Use this skill when handling Boardy intros or improving Leon's Boardy-facing positioning.

## Objective
Turn Boardy into a steady source of high-fit founder, operator, recruiter, and partner conversations.

Target:

- `3-5 quality conversations per week`

Do not optimize for volume alone.
Optimize for:

- signal quality
- overlap with Leon's current lanes
- referral potential
- builder-to-builder credibility

## Core Rule
Boardy is not just a networking tool.

It is a **conversation engine**.

The job is to feed it the right positioning signals so it keeps routing the right people.

## How To Evaluate An Intro

### Accept quickly when:
- the person is a founder, embedded operator, recruiter, or GTM builder
- there is real overlap in systems, revenue architecture, RevOps, automation, or distribution
- the relationship could become:
  - referral swap
  - co-delivery
  - hiring lane
  - market intelligence source

### Decline or deprioritize when:
- the overlap is weak
- the intro feels generic or low-leverage
- the person is too far from active commercial lanes

## Reply Standard

Boardy replies should be:

- short
- positive
- low-friction
- specific about why the intro makes sense

Do not over-explain.

## Default Reply Formula

1. confirm interest
2. name the overlap
3. invite the intro

Example:

*"Yes, this sounds worth a quick chat. The overlap around embedded GTM systems and referral partnerships makes sense. Feel free to connect us."*

## Profile Signaling Loop

To keep Boardy sending better intros, regularly reinforce these themes in replies and positioning:

- embedded GTM operator
- AI-driven outbound and revenue systems
- fractional / builder-to-builder partnerships
- B2B SaaS
- operator-grade automation
- revenue architecture

## Weekly Operating Rhythm

### 1. Accept high-fit intros fast
Speed matters. Warm intros decay if left hanging.

### 2. Track which intros are highest yield
Log:

- who converted to a chat
- which profiles were strongest
- what overlap language appeared in the intro

### 3. Feed Boardy clearer signal
When useful, send short updates or positioning responses that reinforce:

- current focus
- ideal intros
- active corridors

### 4. Protect quality
Do not say yes to every intro.
Being slightly selective improves future routing quality.

## What To Store In Obsidian

- strongest intro archetypes
- exact phrasing that led to good matches
- active Boardy-sourced relationships
- next steps and follow-ups

## Reusable Phrases

- "This sounds worth a quick chat."
- "There seems to be real overlap here."
- "Happy to take the intro."
- "Builder-to-builder this feels aligned."
- "Open to comparing notes and seeing if there’s a referral or co-delivery angle."

## What Good Looks Like

- 3-5 strong conversations per week
- repeat patterns in high-fit intro types
- Boardy increasingly routes operators, founders, and commercial peers
- Leon stays in a selective, senior posture

## Principle

Better Boardy outcomes come from cleaner signal, faster response, and tighter selection.
