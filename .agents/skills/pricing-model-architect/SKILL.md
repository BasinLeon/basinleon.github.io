# Pricing Model Architect

## Use when
The user wants to structure a commercial model, negotiate pricing, build out tiered engagement models for clients, or determine the correct strategic compensation structure for partnerships (including Middle East expansion, equity negotiations, or fractional roles). 

## Core Philosophy
1. **Never Sell Time, Sell Architecture/Outcomes**: Shift the conversation away from hourly rates and toward structural transformation and revenue yield.
2. **The 3-Option Rule**: Always provide three distinct tiers or options. This prevents "yes or no" thinking and encourages "which one" decision-making. (The classic "Decoy Effect" or "Good, Better, Best" framework).
3. **Capture Upside**: Always include a backend (equity, revenue share, performance bonus) to align incentives with the scale of the client's success.
4. **Middle East / Global GTM Connections**: Standardize high-value geopolitical/market-entry negotiations. Structure JV (Joint Venture), strategic board roles, and exclusive affiliate network distribution mechanics separate from standard operational labor.

## The Frameworks

### 1. The Fractional Architect (Retainer + Backend)
*For stable, predictable runway while securing upside.*
- **Base Retainer**: Secures operational bandwidth, system architecture, and focus. (e.g., $2k - $5k/mo).
- **Commitment**: Defined hours or specific weekly sprint allocations (e.g., 5-10 hours/week).
- **Backend Yield**: 5-10% of revenue generated or closed-won commissions from built pipelines.

### 2. The Strategic Partner / Board Advisor (High Leverage)
*For high-growth scale-ups needing network, authority, and distribution, particularly in foreign markets (e.g., Middle East/MENA).*
- **Positioning**: "I am not an employee; I am a gateway."
- **Base Fee**: Advisory retainer ($3k-$8k/mo) or 0.5% - 2% Advisory Equity.
- **Scope**: opening strategic doors, architecting the GTM engine, brokering regional partnerships. Zero day-to-day execution.

### 3. The Outcome-Based / Affiliate Engine (Pure Performance)
*Best for scaling established demand.*
- **Upfront**: Zero or minimal setup fee.
- **Backend Yield**: 15% - 20% flat revenue share on closed business, network referrals, or marketplace transactions.
- **Leverage**: The user utilizes internal closed-loop systems (Bounty.ai, Nexus local agents) to fulfill the pipeline at a lower cost than the revenue generated.

## Execution Rules
- **Name the Tiers**: Give them high-status names (e.g., "The Strategic Growth Architect", "The Scale Engine", "The Partnership Sandbox").
- **Clear Boundaries**: Explicitly state what is *not* included.
- **Anchor High**: The highest tier should represent significant value and carry a high price tag to make closing the mid-tier an easy decision.
