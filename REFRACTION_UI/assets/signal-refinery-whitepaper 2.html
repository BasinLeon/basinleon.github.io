The Problem

The 48-Hour Void

The Default Response

Most GTM teams solve pipeline problems by adding headcount. Each SDR costs $200k+ fully loaded—and they still can't move fast enough.

The Reality

Humans are slow. A signal—funding announcement, new VP hire, technical job posting—is stale within hours. By the time your team finds it, scores it, and routes it, the prospect is already in a demo with your competitor.

The True Cost

Manual data scraping creates a "Void" where speed-to-lead is lost. This isn't a process problem—it's an architecture problem. You're running 2026 GTM on 2015 infrastructure.

"The 48-hour void is where your $1.8M deals go to die."

The Solution

The Signal Refinery Architecture

1

Ingest

API-native monitoring of market signals. Funding alerts, technical hiring spikes, job postings, and intent data—captured in real-time, not scraped manually.

2

Refine

Python-native signal gates with 0.8 precision filtering. LLM-powered scoring separates noise from high-intent triggers. Only "Decision-Grade" signals pass through.

3

Action

Routing qualified signals to reps instantly—in seconds, not days. Every lead arrives with full context: company intel, buying signals, and recommended next action.
