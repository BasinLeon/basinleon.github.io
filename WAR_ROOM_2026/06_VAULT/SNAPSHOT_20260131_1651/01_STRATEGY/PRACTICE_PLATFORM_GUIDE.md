### **1. Pitch Practice** üé§
**What it does:**
- Practice elevator pitches (30s, 60s, 2min, 5min)
- Record and review your pitches
- Timing practice with countdown timer
- Self-assessment framework
- Tips and feedback

**How to use:**
1. Click "Start Practicing"
2. Select pitch type (30s, 60s, etc.)

### **2. Interview Practice** üíº
**What it does:**
- 100+ interview questions
- Behavioral, technical, leadership questions
- Answer recording & timing
- STAR method guidance
- Self-assessment checklist

**How to use:**
1. Click "Start Practicing"
2. Select a question
3. Click "Start Answering"
4. Record or write your answer
5. Check STAR method usage
6. Self-assess and improve

**Question Categories:**
- Behavioral ("Tell me about a time...")
- Motivation ("Why do you want to work here?")
- Leadership ("What's your leadership style?")
- Technical (role-specific)
- Closing ("Why should we hire you?")

**Tips:**
- Use STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
- Quantify results
- Practice 3-5 questions daily
- Record yourself answering

### **4. 90-Day Plan Builder** üìÖ
**What it does:**
- Structured 30/60/90 day templates
- Goal setting framework
- Activity planning
- Success metrics
- Export functionality

**How to use:**
1. Click "Start Building"
2. Fill in each phase:
   - **Days 1-30:** Learn & Assess
   - **Days 31-60:** Build & Execute
   - **Days 61-90:** Scale & Optimize
3. Set goals, activities, metrics for each phase
4. Write summary
5. Export plan

**Framework:**
- **Phase 1 (Days 1-30):** Understanding role, team, processes
- **Phase 2 (Days 31-60):** Taking ownership, implementing improvements
- **Phase 3 (Days 61-90):** Scaling what works, demonstrating impact

**Tips:**
- Be specific with goals
- Set measurable metrics
- Align with company objectives
- Prepare to present your plan

### **5. Negotiation Practice** ü§ù
**What it does:**
- Salary negotiation scenarios
- Deal closing practice
- Objection handling
- Role-play scenarios
- Negotiation framework

**How to use:**
1. Click "Start Practicing"
2. Review scenarios
3. Write your responses
4. Practice out loud
5. Refine your approach

**Scenarios:**
- Salary negotiation
- Deal closing ("we need to think about it")
- Objection handling ("price is too high")

**Framework:**
1. Prepare (research, know your value)
2. Listen (understand their constraints)
3. Propose (anchor high)
4. Negotiate (find creative solutions)
5. Close (confirm agreement)

**Tips:**
- Research market rates
- Know your walk-away point
- Be collaborative, not adversarial
- Find win-win solutions
