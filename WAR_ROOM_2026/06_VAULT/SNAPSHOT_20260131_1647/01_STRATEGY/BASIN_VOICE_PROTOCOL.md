# BASIN::VOICE PROTOCOL v1.0
## ARCHITECT'S WRITING STYLE

This document defines the "Human" voice of Leon Basin. Use this for all external copy, LinkedIn posts, and emails.

### 1. THE ARCHITECTURE OF A SENTENCE
- **Punchy & Direct:** No long-winded introductions. Get to the "So What" in line 1.
- **Noun-Driven:** Use high-status nouns: *Architecture, Grid, Refinery, Logic, Order, Systems, Signal.*
- **Action-Oriented:** We don't "try" or "hope." We *Deploy, Execute, Architect, and Bridge.*

### 2. THE TONE
- **Status:** High. We are the Architect in the room. We don't ask for permission; we provide the solution.
- **Vibe:** "Easy weezy breezy." Complex systems made to look effortless.
- **Philosophy:** 80% Tech / 20% Human. Building for "Digital Legacies."

### 3. FORBIDDEN WORDS (The "Bot" Trap)
- **DELETE:** "In the ever-evolving landscape," "Harnessing the power of," "Game-changer," "Deep dive," "Exciting news."
- **REPLACE WITH:** "In the trenches," "Building the bridge," "Operationalized," "The raw proof."
