The Problem with Traditional Outreach

Most outbound scripts sound like they were written by someone who's never talked to an engineer. Generic pain points. Buzzword salad. Zero technical credibility. Engineers can smell fake technical empathy from a mile away.

Lead Script Generator solves this by analyzing your prospect's actual technical stack, identifying friction points in their workflow, and generating personalized sequences that speak engineer-to-engineer.

No "touching base." No "circling back." Just specific, technical observations about problems they're likely solving right now.

Example Output

Subject: Your Kubernetes setup at [Company] - observability gap?

Hey [Name],

Noticed you're running K8s on GKE (saw your Medium post on autoscaling). If you're like most teams managing 20+ microservices, you're probably fighting two things:

1. Prometheus cardinality explosions

How It Works

1

Input Target Profile

Provide LinkedIn URL, GitHub profile, or company tech stack. The tool scrapes public data to understand their technical context.

2

Friction Analysis

LLM analyzes their stack and identifies likely pain points. Example: If they use Jenkins + Docker, they're probably fighting CI/CD pipeline bottlenecks.

3

Generate Sequence

Creates 3-5 touch sequence with escalating specificity. Each message references real technical details from their public profile.

4

Human Review & Send

Outputs to CSV or directly to your CRM. Review for accuracy, add personal touches, then send. AI does research, you add authenticity.
