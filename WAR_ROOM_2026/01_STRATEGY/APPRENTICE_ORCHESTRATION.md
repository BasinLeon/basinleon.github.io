To achieve $1M ARR as a solo operator, Leon Basin (The Principal) leverages the Architect to train and deploy **Apprentice Agents**. These are discrete, task-specific scripts that handle the "Inhale" and "Exhale" functions of the business.

---

## TRAINING PROTOCOL

1.  **Skill Acquisition:** Architect writes the core logic.
2.  **Shadowing:** Apprentice runs in "Observe Mode," logging what it *would* have done.
3.  **Correction:** Principal/Architect reviews logs and adjusts logic gates.
4.  **Deployment:** Apprentice runs autonomously via cron or event-triggers.
