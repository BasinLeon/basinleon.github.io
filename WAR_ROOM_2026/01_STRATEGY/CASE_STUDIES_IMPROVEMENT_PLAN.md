## üéØ Best Practices from Executive Portfolios

### Structure (What Top Executives Do):
1. **Hero Section** - Project name, company, role, timeframe
2. **The Challenge** - Problem statement, constraints, context
3. **My Role** - What you did, team size, responsibilities
4. **The Process** - How you approached it, methodology
5. **The Solution** - What you built, architecture, systems
6. **The Results** - Metrics, impact, testimonials
7. **Key Learnings** - What you learned, what you'd do differently

### Visual Elements:
- Before/after metrics (charts, graphs)
- Process diagrams
- Architecture diagrams
- Screenshots of tools/systems
- Testimonials with photos/logos

### Content Depth:
- **Minimum:** 1,500-2,000 words per case study
- **Ideal:** 2,500-4,000 words
- **Sections:** 5-7 major sections
- **Metrics:** 3-5 key metrics per case
