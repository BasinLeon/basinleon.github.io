Overview

BASIN::NEXUS is a comprehensive career intelligence platform built for GTM professionals who refuse to be passive job seekers. It's not a resume builder or another job board scraper‚Äîit's an operating system for career execution.

Born from frustration with generic career tools, BASIN::NEXUS combines AI-powered automation with tactical execution frameworks. It doesn't just help you find jobs‚Äîit helps you systematically dominate the interview process.

Built entirely in Python/Streamlit with 5 LLM integrations (Claude, GPT-4, Gemini, Mixtral, Llama), it represents 8 months of development and real-world testing across 47 job applications.

The Problem

Most job seekers are reactive: they scroll LinkedIn, mass-apply, hope for callbacks, and wing interviews. This approach works for junior roles. It fails for Director/VP positions where hiring managers expect systems thinkers, not seat-fillers.

The Solution

BASIN::NEXUS treats career search like a GTM campaign: signal detection ‚Üí systematic qualification ‚Üí structured prep ‚Üí execution. Every feature is designed to multiply your human effort with AI, not replace your judgment.
