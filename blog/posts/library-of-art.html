A Library of Art, A Foundation of Words

Every book I've ever loved taught me something about how to see the world differently. This is a reflection on building a personal library—not just of books, but of ideas that shape how I work.

The best libraries aren't just collections. They're conversations across time. A cybersecurity manual next to a poetry anthology. A sales playbook next to philosophy. The juxtaposition creates new connections.

As I close out 2024, I'm building two kinds of archives: one digital (this blog, my code repositories), and one physical (the bookshelf that frames my thinking). Both matter. Both compound.

The goal for 2025: write more, read deeper, and let the library grow in ways that surprise me.
