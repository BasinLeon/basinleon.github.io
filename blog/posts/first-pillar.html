Unveiling the First Pillar

Building an effective GTM engine requires solid foundations. This is Pillar One: the infrastructure layer that everything else depends on.

What the First Pillar includes:

1. Data architecture: Clean, unified data that flows between systems. If your CRM is a mess, everything downstream fails.

2. Process documentation: Playbooks that anyone can follow. The knowledge shouldn't be in people's heads‚Äîit should be in the system.

3. Measurement framework: KPIs that actually matter, tracked consistently. You can't improve what you don't measure.

Most GTM failures happen at the foundation level. Teams skip to tactics before building infrastructure. They optimize campaigns before fixing data quality. They hire SDRs before defining process.

The First Pillar isn't glamorous. But without it, nothing else works.
