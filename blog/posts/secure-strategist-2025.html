The Secure Strategist: Celebrating Our Growth and Looking Forward to 2025

Thank you to everyone who's been reading The Secure Strategist. This newsletter started as an experiment in thinking out loud about cybersecurity, GTM strategy, and the intersection of both.

If you're building at the intersection of security and growth, I'd love to connect. The best insights come from conversation.

Onward.
