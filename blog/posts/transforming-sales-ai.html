Transforming Sales: AI and Automation's Impact

The sales floor of 2024 looks nothing like 2014. AI and automation have fundamentally changed what it means to sell—and what it means to be a seller.

What's changed: Prospecting is automated. Data enrichment happens in real-time. Conversation intelligence surfaces patterns human managers would miss. CRMs are becoming intelligent systems, not just databases.

What hasn't: Relationships still close deals. Trust still matters. The best sellers are still deeply curious about their customers' problems.

The new imperative: The sellers who thrive are the ones who use AI as leverage, not a crutch. They automate the routine to make room for the relational. They let machines handle volume while they handle nuance.

This is the future I'm building toward with Basin::Nexus: systems that amplify human judgment, not replace it.
