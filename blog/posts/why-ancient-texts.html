Why Ancient Texts Keep Outliving New Technology

PhilosophyAI

Every generation thinks its tools are unprecedented. Ancient texts survive not because they predict the future, but because they understand formation.

When technology advances faster than wisdom, we don’t need new prophecies. We need older grammars. Creation without responsibility fractures; power without structure destabilizes.
