Product Intelligence
Notes from First Session — March 31, 2026
Pricing

Drop Starter to $20/mo. Current entry price creates unnecessary friction for the Gen Z/early-career rep segment. Lower the floor, prove retention, then move up-market with confidence.

Coaching Layer

Add post-call analytics. Pace, filler word count, talk-to-listen ratio. Read.ai does this post-call — Winzly's live version of this is a material differentiator that doesn't exist elsewhere.

Onboarding

Keypad gap is a quick win. When making outbound calls, the keypad should surface automatically. Fix before next demo — it's the first friction point a new rep hits.

Channel Expansion

Add Google Meet. A large segment of early-stage and SMB teams never left Meet. Zoom + Teams + Meet = full coverage of the virtual sales stack.

Architecture Risk

Vendor lock-in on OpenAI. When OpenAI releases its own sales automation layer, they become both infrastructure and competitor. Plan the Llama migration path now — don't build it yet.

Channel Strategy

X + TikTok for zero-cost acquisition. Builders and SDRs both live on X. TikTok reaches the Gen Z rep entering sales. Content-driven growth before paying for ads.
