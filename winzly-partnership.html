Winzly

Most coaching tools review the call after the deal is already dead. This is the GTM model to get Winzly to 1,000 active users — built from the first conversation.

Prepared by Leon Basin
Date March 31, 2026
Target 1,000 Users / EOY
The Core Thesis

Gong tells you what happened after the deal dies. Winzly fixes the conversation while it's still happening.

That's not a feature difference — that's a category difference. The ghosting problem doesn't happen in the CRM. It happens on the call, in the moment a rep hesitates or fumbles an objection and the prospect mentally checks out. Winzly is the only tool that addresses it in real time.

This is the working model to get Winzly positioned, distributed, and to 1,000 active users.

Execution Framework
30 / 60 / 90 Activation Model
PHASE 01
Foundation & Signal
Days 1–30
  • I deploy Winzly on my own calls — TalSmart sessions, MTI coaching, live consulting. Document what lands and what breaks.
  • Drop the entry price to $20/mo. Lower the floor, get volume, prove retention, then move up-market.
  • 3–5 warm intros to SDR/BDR team leads in my network. Qualified intros with context, not cold blasts.
  • One-pager for the RevOps buyer. They don't speak the same language as sales managers. Untapped entry point.
  • Two quick product fixes: keypad on live outbound calls, Google Meet integration scoped.
PHASE 02
GTM Layer & Partnerships
Days 31–60
  • Co-branded GTM playbook — how SDR teams at Series A/B companies should deploy Winzly. A sales asset for Mena, proof-of-work for Leon.
  • Advisor structure locked. Referral attribution, commission terms, equity scope. Formalized.
  • SCU Guest Lecture (May 12) — Leon presents Winzly to MBA students and business operators. Real audience, zero cost.
  • Content engine on X and LinkedIn. 2–3 posts positioning Winzly as the live layer between CRM data and conversation. Leon publishes, Mena amplifies.
  • University sales programs. Onboard Gen Z reps before they enter the workforce — cheap acquisition, long retention tail.
PHASE 03
Scale & Capital Readiness
Days 61–90
  • Push to 1,000 users via network-driven acquisition. Package retention data for the pre-seed investor narrative.
  • TalSmart referral arrangement. Their clients are exactly Winzly's ICP — tech sales teams that need live coaching infrastructure.
  • First AE hire profile if pre-seed closes. Leon defines the spec and first-rep sales motion.
  • Model migration plan. OpenAI becoming both infrastructure and competitor is the real risk. Map the Llama path now, execute when ready.
  • Coaching analytics layer scoped — pace, filler words, talk-to-listen ratio. Read.ai does this post-call. Winzly does it live.
Product Intelligence
Notes from Session One — March 31, 2026
Pricing

Drop Starter to $20/mo. Current price creates friction for Gen Z and early-career reps. Lower the floor, prove retention, move up-market.

Coaching Layer

Add post-call analytics. Pace, filler words, talk-to-listen ratio. Read.ai has this post-call — Winzly live is a differentiator that doesn't exist anywhere else.

Onboarding

Keypad gap is a quick win. When making outbound calls, the keypad should surface automatically. Fix before the next demo.

Channel

Add Google Meet. A large segment of early-stage and SMB teams never left Meet. Zoom + Teams + Meet = full virtual sales stack coverage.

Architecture Risk

Vendor lock-in on OpenAI. When they release their own sales layer, they become both infrastructure and competitor. Plan the Llama migration path now.

Channel Strategy

X + TikTok for zero-cost acquisition. Builders and SDRs both live on X. TikTok reaches Gen Z entering sales. Content before paid ads.
