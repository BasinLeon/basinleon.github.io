Real-time AI coaching is the missing layer between CRM data and live conversation. This is the GTM architecture to get Winzly to 1,000 active users — and beyond.

Prepared by Leon Basin
Date March 31, 2026
Status Active — Pending Next Session
Target 1,000 Users / EOY
The core thesis: Most sales coaching tools are post-mortem — they review what went wrong after the call ends. Winzly solves the problem in the moment it happens. That's a fundamentally different architecture, and it maps directly to where pipeline velocity breaks down: the live conversation, not the CRM report.

Basin::Nexus brings the GTM infrastructure layer — signal routing, ICP positioning, channel strategy, and network distribution — to accelerate Winzly's path to product-market fit.
Execution Framework
30 / 60 / 90 Activation Model
PHASE 01
Foundation & Signal
Days 1–30
  • Active User Testing: Leon deploys Winzly across live consulting and sales training sessions (TalSmart, MTI) — documenting friction points and high-signal moments in real B2B conversations.
  • Pricing Wedge: Drop Starter to $20/mo to reduce friction for Gen Z and early-career reps. Volume first, then move up-market.
  • Warm Network Intros: 3–5 direct introductions to contacts actively running SDR/BDR teams — qualified warm intros with context, not cold blasts.
  • GTM Positioning One-Pager: Build dedicated positioning for the RevOps/GTM buyer. They speak a different language than sales managers — that's an untapped segment.
  • Quick Product Wins: Fix the keypad gap on live calls, scope Google Meet integration, surface the onboarding friction points.
PHASE 02
GTM Layer & Partnerships
Days 31–60
  • Co-Branded GTM Playbook: How SDR teams at Series A/B companies should onboard and deploy Winzly — a sales asset for Mena, proof-of-work for Leon.
  • Advisor Structure: Define referral attribution, commission terms, and advisor equity scope. Formalize the relationship.
  • SCU Guest Lecture (May 12): Leon presents Winzly to a room of MBA students and business operators. Real audience, zero acquisition cost.
  • Content Engine: 2–3 posts on X and LinkedIn positioning Winzly as the missing layer between CRM data and live conversation. Leon publishes, Mena amplifies.
  • University Sales Programs: Target Gen Z reps before they enter the workforce — onboard them cheap, retain them as they move into corporate roles.
PHASE 03
Scale & Capital Readiness
Days 61–90
  • 1,000 User Milestone: Push past the threshold with network-driven acquisition. Package retention data for pre-seed investor narrative.
  • TalSmart Integration: TalSmart's clients are exactly Winzly's ICP — tech sales teams and enterprise training orgs. Evaluate a formal referral arrangement.
  • First AE Hire Profile: If pre-seed closes, Leon defines the GTM hire spec and first-rep sales motion.
  • Model Architecture Review: Once PMF signals are clear, evaluate migration path toward open-source LLMs (Llama/Meta) to reduce OpenAI vendor dependency.
  • Coaching Analytics Layer: Post-call metrics — talk pace, filler words, talk-to-listen ratio. Read.ai does this post-call. Winzly does it live. That's the differentiator.
Product Intelligence
Notes from Session One — March 31, 2026
Pricing

Drop Starter to $20/mo. Current entry price creates friction for the Gen Z/early-career rep segment. Lower the floor, prove retention, then move up-market.

Coaching Layer

Add post-call analytics. Pace, filler word count, talk-to-listen ratio. Read.ai does this post-call — Winzly live is a material differentiator that doesn't exist elsewhere.

Onboarding

Keypad gap is a quick win. When making outbound calls, the keypad should surface automatically. Fix before next demo — it's the first friction point a new rep hits.

Channel

Add Google Meet. A large segment of early-stage and SMB teams never left Meet. Zoom + Teams + Meet = full coverage of the virtual sales stack.

Architecture Risk

Vendor lock-in on OpenAI. When OpenAI releases its own sales layer, they become both infrastructure and competitor. Plan the Llama migration path now — don't build it yet.

Channel Strategy

X + TikTok for zero-cost acquisition. Builders and SDRs both live on X. TikTok reaches Gen Z entering sales. Content-driven growth before paying for ads.
