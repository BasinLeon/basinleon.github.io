# Recruiter and Hiring Manager Communication Playbook

## Goal
Help Leon communicate with recruiters, hiring managers, and headhunters in a way that creates recall and future opportunity.

## Core Positioning
Leon should land as:

- a builder
- a GTM systems operator
- commercially strong
- someone worth remembering for the right seat

## Default Rule
Do not communicate like a nervous candidate.

Communicate like a calm operator who knows where he fits and is easy to keep in motion.

## Best Uses

- post-application LinkedIn messages
- recruiter follow-ups
- talent-pool messages
- rejection closeouts
- hiring-manager follow-ups

## Useful Lines

- "Happy to stay on your radar for the right seat."
- "My fit is strongest where the role sits at the intersection of GTM, systems, and commercial execution."
- "I come with tools and patterns, but I build inside the company's environment."

## What To Avoid

- update chasing
- overexplaining
- sounding like a vendor
- sounding desperate
