## 1. The "Logic Gap" Alignment
TryBounty provides the V2 Execution Layer (Sourcing -> Enrichment -> Verification -> Outreach).

I do not sell them *tools*. I sell them the *Outcome Architecture* ($20k pipeline, 90% hold rates, beating competitors). They must use Bounty to run my architecture.

## 2. The Commercial Partnership Model

### Tier 1: The Architect's "Execution License" (Credit-Based Affiliate)
Instead of a simple "referral link" (which is low-status and commodity), we run a **Consumption-Based Affiliate/Credit Rev-Share**.
- **The Deal:** Every time Leon sets up a client (a Node) on the Basin::Nexus architecture using TryBounty as the core S2Y (Signal-to-Yield) engine, Leon gets a rev-share OR massive platform credit injections.
- **The Mechanic:** The client pays Bounty full price for a massive enterprise plan, and Leon receives a 20% ongoing cut of their Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) or equivilent credit volume to scale his own Master Engine.
- **The Pitch:** *"I don't just send you leads. I enforce adoption. None of my clients know how to use your platform out of the box. They use it because I architect their entire business around it. That stickiness guarantees you 0% churn on my referred seats. In exchange, I take a 20% cut of the lifetime value."*

### Tier 2: The "Ghost Mirror" Agency License (Whitelabel/Co-Sell)
As TryBounty builds its V2 Execution Layer, Leon gets exclusive Alpha/Beta access to stress-test workflows on live 5-figure deals (e.g., Ether AI, Haste, Jassi).
- **The Deal:** Leon uses Bounty to close his own consulting deals. Bounty gives Leon unlimited (or massively discounted) credits for his own "Architect Hub" in exchange for the live feedback loops and case studies he brings them from enterprise deployments.
- **The Pitch:** *"I am dropping your V2 into a $20k multifamily construction pipeline tomorrow. You give me the unlimited ammo to fire; I will give you the precise telemetry on where your execution layer breaks, and I will publish a case study on how we beat Clay so you can use it for your own marketing."*

### Tier 3: GTM Fractional Partnership
If Bounty wants to truly exit the dataset commodity game, they need 1M-tier GTM logic built into their own outbound engine.
- **The Deal:** Leon consults directly for Bounty to architect *their* sales pipeline using the exact same framework he brings to Executive Search and Construction clients, on a fractional logic retainer or equity-driven basis.
