# Basin::Nexus | AI Interview Platform GTM Architecture

## The Context
During the Friday, March 13 (2026) review, we mapped the AI Interview Platform. The system natively handles:
1. CV parsing & contextual interview synthesis.
2. Generating phased, role-specific questions.
3. Post-interview CV optimization & alignment score.

**Current State Metrics:**
- **Users:** ~243 registered.
- **Traction:** ~47 assessments, 17 full interviews taken.
- **Feedback Loop:** 15-20 written feedback responses relying on native form input.

## 1. Competitive Positioning & "The Wedge"
The EdTech & Interview Prep space is currently flooded with generic AI resume parsers (like Jobscan) and basic mock interview bots (TLHQ).
Our true differentiator isn't just the AI—it's **The Human Interviewer Marketplace Sandbox**. We are positioning the platform not as an "AI prep tool" but as a **comprehensive vetting and feedback loop** that intelligently filters candidates *before* passing them to a human evaluator. 

**The Message:** "We don't replace human interviewers; we weed out the noise so human evaluators only spend time on ready candidates."

## 2. 30/60/90 Day Execution Plan (Target: 1,000 Users)

### Days 1-30: Foundation & Frictionless Feedback
*Objective: Build the core data loop and stabilize early engagement.*
- **KPI Target:** 100 Active End-to-End Users.
- **Product Loop:** Replace heavy text feedback blocks with a "Frictionless Survey" (1-10 NPS + 1 optional text field) triggered post-interview. Provide an immediate incentive (e.g., "Unlock Advanced Market Analysis") in exchange for feedback.
- **Outbound Signal Targeting (Nexus Engine):** Target immediate intent—Entry-level tech talent, recent bootcamp graduates, and recently laid-off juniors via LinkedIn Sales Navigator. 
- **Content Engine:** Ship 2 weekly posts on LinkedIn emphasizing "Why generic AI fails your resumes" vs. "Our phased, context-aware AI interviewer."

### Days 31-60: The Partnership Sprints
*Objective: Shift from B2C grinding to B2B2C wholesale distribution.*
- **KPI Target:** 400 Active End-to-End Users.
- **Affiliate/Partnership Targets:** Coding Bootcamps (General Assembly, Springboard, BloomTech) and University Career Centers.
- **The "Land and Expand" Hook:** "Give our tool to your students/cohorts for free; your career services team gets the backend analytics dashboard to see who is actually applying and practicing."
- **Marketplace Seeding:** Begin aggressively vetting and onboarding the first 5-10 senior industry professionals for the paid human-interviewer marketplace tier.

### Days 61-90: Viral Mechanics & Enterprise Scale
*Objective: Systematize referrals and open the enterprise pipeline.*
- **KPI Target:** 1,000 Active End-to-End Users.
- **Growth Loops:** Implement "Refer a friend to unlock a 30-minute Human Mock Interview session."
- **Inbound Engine:** Launch a free "Roast My Resume" lead magnet that automatically funnels traffic into a required mock interview.
- **Enterprise Pivot (SaaS):** Begin pitching Bootcamps on a white-labeled or fully integrated SaaS version of the platform for a recurring MRR (e.g., $500/mo).
