# Basin::Nexus | Bounty.ai Agency Affiliate Blueprint

## The Wedge: Outcome-Based Revenue
Bounty.ai is shifting demand from SaaS subscriptions (ZoomInfo, Apollo) to an **outcome-based agent model** (paying only for successful outcomes like lead sourcing, validation, and enrichment).

**The Moat:** Agencies and GTM teams are burning cash on static data vendors. Moving them to an outcome-based model is a high-status play. As an orchestrator and fraction GTM leader, Basin::Nexus acts as the bridge.

## 1. The Affiliate Architecture (Win-Win Mechanics)
Based on conversations with Shivam, Rayan, and Aashna (Bounty Founders):

### The "Credit Rebate" Flywheel
- **The Hook:** We onboard marketing and GTM agencies to Bounty.ai to replace their static data tooling overhead.
- **The Mechanic:** For every dollar ($X) an onboarded agency spends on bounties, Bounty deposits a percentage (e.g., $X/5 = 20%) back into the Basin::Nexus orchestrator account as platform credits.
- **The Application:** We utilize those credits to power our *own* proprietary GTM engines (or client sprints) effectively making our data acquisition **cost-free**.

## 2. Phase 1: Local Engine Verification (Current)
*Objective: Prove the execution speed and quality of Bounty's Demand-side.*
1. **Initial testing:** Expend the $5.00 in initial credits on targeted Sourcing & Enrichment bounties.
2. **Intern Swarm:** Onboard SCU interns into the Bounty environment. Have them run GTM sprints posting outcomes instead of manually scraping data.
3. **Yield Verification:** Measure the time-to-lead and data accuracy of the agents completing the bounties vs. our previous manual tools.

## 3. Phase 2: Agency Ascension (The Hitlist)
*Objective: Migrate 3 target agencies onto the platform to activate the Credit Rebate.*
We will design a custom Agency Onboarding flow:
1. **The Audit:** We analyze a target agency's current tech stack spend (e.g., $20k/yr on ZoomInfo).
2. **The Pilot:** We run a parallel sprint using Bounty to prove the cost-per-outcome is drastically lower and higher quality.
3. **The Migration:** We use our affiliate tracking structure to migrate their outbound ops, locking in the 20% credit kickback.

## 4. Next Steps & Agentic Swarm Orchestration
As the central architect, I will begin structuring the target list of agencies. We will deploy local agents (via our new `nexus_local_agent.py`) to scrape signals from our network of agency founders, vet them for intent, and assemble the outreach payloads.
