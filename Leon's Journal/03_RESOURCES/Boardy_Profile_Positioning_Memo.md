# Boardy Profile Positioning Memo

## Objective
Increase the number and quality of Boardy intros by making Leon's positioning easier for Boardy to route against.

Target outcome:

- `3-5 quality conversations per week`

## Primary Positioning
Leon should read as:

- an embedded GTM operator
- a builder of AI-driven outbound and revenue systems
- a fractional revenue architect for B2B SaaS
- a strong fit for builder-to-builder partnerships, referrals, and co-delivery

## Short Positioning Block
Use this as the default compressed signal:

> I build AI-driven outbound and revenue systems for B2B SaaS teams as an embedded GTM operator. Strong fit for founder conversations, fractional GTM work, referral partnerships, and builder-to-builder operator exchanges.

## Longer Positioning Block
Use this when a fuller signal is useful:

> I work as an embedded GTM operator and revenue architect for B2B SaaS teams, building outbound systems, automation layers, and operator-grade revenue infrastructure. My strongest overlap is with founders, fractional leaders, RevOps builders, recruiters, and referral partners where there is room for co-delivery, deal swaps, or strategic collaboration.

## Ideal Intros

### Highest priority
- founders with distribution or GTM friction
- embedded / fractional GTM leaders
- RevOps operators
- recruiters for builder/operator roles
- partners with referral or co-delivery upside

### Good secondary intros
- product-minded operators with commercial ownership
- agencies or advisors with overlapping B2B SaaS clients
- AI builders with real go-to-market responsibility

## Weak Intros To Avoid
- generic networkers
- vague business coaches
- broad "growth hackers"
- people with no clear commercial overlap
- introductions that only sound inspirational but not useful

## Keywords to Reinforce

- embedded GTM operator
- revenue architecture
- AI-driven outbound
- revenue systems
- B2B SaaS
- fractional GTM
- builder-to-builder
- referral partnership
- co-delivery
- operator-grade automation

## Best Conversation Angles

- compare GTM operating systems
- share referral opportunities
- discuss embedded engagement models
- swap playbooks around pipeline visibility, outbound, RevOps, and automation
- evaluate whether there is a clean partner lane

## What Boardy Should Understand
Leon is not looking for random networking calls.

Leon is highest-value when routed toward:

- people building or fixing revenue systems
- operator peers
- founders with GTM urgency
- commercial partners who can exchange signal and deal flow

## Reusable Boardy Reply Style
Short and selective.

Example:

> Yes, this sounds worth a quick chat. There seems to be real overlap here around embedded GTM systems and builder-to-builder partnership potential. Feel free to connect us.

## Signal Maintenance Rule
Whenever replying to Boardy or Boardy-sourced intros, reinforce one or two of these frames:

- embedded GTM systems
- AI-driven revenue infrastructure
- builder-to-builder partnership
- referral potential

That keeps the routing engine calibrated.
