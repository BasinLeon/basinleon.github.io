# Boardy Conversation Engine Playbook

## Goal
Use Boardy to generate `3-5 quality conversations per week`.

Not random chats.
Not weak networking.

The target is:

- founders
- embedded operators
- recruiters
- referral partners
- builder-to-builder peers

## Positioning Signal to Reinforce
Leon should consistently appear as:

- an embedded GTM operator
- builder of AI-driven outbound and revenue systems
- a fractional revenue architect
- a strong fit for referral partnerships and co-delivery

## What Makes a Strong Boardy Intro

- clear overlap in GTM systems or revenue operations
- immediate builder-to-builder credibility
- commercial adjacency
- partnership or hiring upside

## Default Reply Style
Short, interested, selective.

The ideal Boardy reply:

1. says yes or no quickly
2. names the overlap
3. makes the intro easy

## Weekly Loop

1. accept high-fit intros quickly
2. log who converted
3. note what phrasing or profile type was strongest
4. refine the positioning signal

## Metrics

- intros received
- intros accepted
- conversations booked
- high-fit conversations
- downstream value:
  - referral
  - opportunity
  - partner lane
  - market intelligence
