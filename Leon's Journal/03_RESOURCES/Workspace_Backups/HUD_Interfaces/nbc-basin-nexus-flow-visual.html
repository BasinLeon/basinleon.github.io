NBC Post-Training Activation

NBC::Activation Flow

This is the visual operating flow behind the NBC post-training layer. It captures demand, cleans the signal, checks what is real, and routes the right opportunities into action for the sales team.

Capture Pull in buyer movement, market changes, intent activity, and inbound interest around digital demand.
Rules Clean the records, score them, and apply simple logic so weak signals do not waste AE time.
Route Send the right record to the right rep or operator with the next step attached.
Learn Track what moved forward, what stalled, and feed that back into the routing rules.
Why This Matters

NBC needs activation, not more delay.

Training improves rep skill, but it does not fix slow intake, weak qualification, or handoff gaps. This visual shows the missing layer between signal and sales action.

Core Translation

The system decides what deserves attention now.

The point is not more leads. The point is knowing what to ignore, what to review, and what to route fast while intent is still live.
