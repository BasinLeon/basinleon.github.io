**Source:** Basin::Nexus & Genie AI Architecture

---

## 1. The Core Leak: Discoverability & Conversion
Currently, you are training 15 AEs, but the digital front door is leaking high-value prospects.

- **Missed Discoverability:** Top-of-funnel intent (VPs of Sales searching for training) isn't being captured early enough.
- **Missed Conversion:** The 48-Hour Tax. When buyers do land, it's a static brochure. They fill out a form and wait.
- **The Gap:** You lose verified intent to competitors who move faster.

---

## 2. The Solution: A Closed-Loop Architecture
We are deploying a **Revenue Activation Layer**. 

Traffic generation and Sales conversion are no longer siloed. We replace your static forms with an autonomous engine designed to detect, qualify, and route prospects in **under 60 seconds**.

---

## 3. Step 1: Upstream Intent Capture (Nexus)
Before they ever reach your form, we know who is looking.

- **The Signal Refinery:** We programmatically identify specific ICP targets.
- **Real-Time Triggers:** We deploy messaging based on funding rounds or executive hiring signals.
- **Result:** Exact target profiles (VPs/Directors) are driven directly to your domain, bypassing general market noise.

---

## 4. Step 2 & 3: Downstream Activation (Regata)
When the prospect lands on your site, the engine takes over.

- **Instant Engagement:** The site adapts instantly. No scrolling for "Contact Us".
- **Voice AI Qualification:** The AI runs the prospect through your specific MEDDIC or BANT framework in under 2 minutes.
- **The Live Bridge:** Qualified opportunities are routed via Slack, instantly connecting the prospect on the website to one of your 15 AEs via **Live Video Call**.

---

## 5. The Outcome: Deterministic Pipeline
This infrastructure guarantees you never lose a high-intent buyer to the "48-hour response tax".

- **Increased Pipeline:** Generate more revenue from existing traffic volume.
- **Operational Efficiency:** Zero additional SDR headcount required.
- **Immediate ROI:** Your 15 AEs engage VP-level buyers explicitly when their intent is at its absolute peak.
