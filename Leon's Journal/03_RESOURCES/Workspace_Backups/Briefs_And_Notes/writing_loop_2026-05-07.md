## Reusable Voice Rule

If the draft exists to frame a meeting, argument, or decision, sentence one should carry the judgment. Cut the recap sentence and make the room react to the real point immediately.

## Short Keep/Avoid Ledger

### Keep

- "My read before Friday..." for operator notes tied to a live meeting.
- "The next step is..." when shifting from diagnosis to action.
- "working review" over "demo" or "presentation" when the goal is pressure-testing.

### Avoid

- "Ahead of Friday's demo..." when the verdict can open.
- abstract scene-setting like "We are in the era of..."
- proof stacks that repeat the diagnosis instead of advancing the decision.
