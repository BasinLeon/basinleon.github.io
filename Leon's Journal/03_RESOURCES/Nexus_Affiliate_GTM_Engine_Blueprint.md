## 2. Expanded Agent Skills for the Ecosystem

To power this standalone GTM OS, I must architect the following newly defined Agent Skills into Basin::Nexus:

### A. The "Affiliate-to-Revenue" Neural Router
* **Purpose:** Process leads generated by passive channels (Udemy, YouTube, Affiliate pages).
* **Operation:** When a lead hits the affiliate link, the Router grabs the email, hits LinkedIn to map their title/company, and autonomously determines if they should receive the "Enterprise Assessment" flow or the "Individual Contributor" flow. 
* **Category Dominance:** Nobody is doing programmatic affiliate routing with live data enrichment.

### B. The 40-Inbound Vetter (Triage Agent)
* **Purpose:** Inbound outreach generated 40+ raw responses from the India campaign. Hand-processing these kills momentum.
* **Operation:** Reads raw inbox responses, categorizes intent (Positive, Deferral, Rejection, Wrong Contact), and instantly drafts the next action using the proprietary TalSmart Playbook logic.
* **Category Dominance:** True "Inbox Zero" for sales teams. The OS manages the entire mid-funnel conversation until a strategic meeting is booked.

### C. The Regulatory/Compliance Trigger
* **Purpose:** Notice "Shalaka Verma" in the raw data stating: *"Nopes, compliance does not allows us to take this :)"*
* **Operation:** The engine flags regulatory friction early. It automatically pivots the conversation from "Take this assessment" to "Enterprise Compliance-ready Executive Briefings." It bypasses the gatekeeper by understanding corporate risk.

### D. The Lateral Pivot Agent (The "Wrong Person" Handler)
* **Purpose:** Handles the classic *"I'm not the right person / We lack budget for this sector"* objection (see Anilkumar Bangari response).
* **Operation:** Acknowledges the specific department ("IT ERP Products"), establishes parity, and sets an automated 90-day trigger to re-engage once their budget opens, while gently asking "Who currently owns standard sales enablement?".
