# Basin::Nexus | Agent Skill Stacking Logic
**Concept:** Moving from "Single Tool" usage to "Outcome Systems."

## The Logic of the "Stack"
We don't just use agents; we **stack** them. A "Deal Closer" stack requires three layers of agentic intervention to overcome the "Logic Gap."

### LAYER 1: The Scout (Signal Discovery)
*   **Agents**: `signal-scout-pro`, `hiringcafe-job-parser`.
*   **Function**: Locating the "Why Now" (Economic Indicators, VC Funding, Hiring Spikes).
*   **Goal**: Find the raw Signal.

### LAYER 2: The Architect (Structural Refinery)
*   **Agents**: `revenue-architect-audit`, `nexus-economic-intelligence`.
*   **Function**: Mapping the signal to a business case.
*   **Goal**: Bridge the "Logic Gap."

### LAYER 3: The Closer (Conversion Layer)
*   **Agents**: `diagnostic-led-outreach`, `nexus-strategic-copy`.
*   **Function**: Generating the high-status lure and the ROI model (HUD).
*   **Goal**: Secure the Sync.
