## 📉 Patterns of Improvement

1.  **From Volume to Signal**: Early scripts (Feb) focused on "publishing" and "scraping" volume. Current skills focus on "intent scoring" and "architect status."
2.  **From Strings to Logic**: Old scripts relied on hardcoded templates. New skills use the **STARK** (Signal, Tension, Architecture, Result, Kinetic) framework to generate dynamic, high-status logic.
3.  **From Desktop to Obsidian**: Work used to live in disconnected `.py` files. It now lives in a **Feedback Loop** centered in your Obsidian Vault.
