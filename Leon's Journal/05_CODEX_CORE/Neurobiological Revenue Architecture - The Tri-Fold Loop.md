## 🏛️ The Tri-Fold Framework
Based on the **TalSmart White Paper (2020)**, we have refactored sales persuasion into three deterministic engineering layers. Instead of "soft skills," we deploy **Neural Agents** to solve specific biological friction points.

### 1. The Instinctual Layer (Reptilian Brain)
- **Goal**: Kill the defenses. Remove the "Vendor Guard."
- **Problem**: 8 out of 10 executives feel meetings are a waste of time (Forrester).
- **Agent Skill**: `neuro-instinct-guard`
- **Logic**: Establish high-status rapport immediately by leading with a **Diagnostic Audit** instead of a pitch. The brain stops protecting and starts collaborating.

### 2. The Emotional Layer (Limbic Brain)
- **Goal**: Pull the right chords. Future-state imagination.
- **Problem**: Long sales cycles (3-18 months) lead to "Mind Fluctuation" and loss of interest.
- **Agent Skill**: `neuro-limbic-resonator`
- **Logic**: Use recurring "Status Lures" and emotional resonance to keep the buyer in love with the *outcome*. Avoiding fear-based tactics which create long-term leakage.

### 3. The Logical Layer (Neocortex Brain)
- **Goal**: Rule out objections. Establish deterministic credibility.
- **Problem**: 58% of buyers feel sales reps can't answer their questions.
- **Agent Skill**: `neuro-logic-validator`
- **Logic**: Deploy hard facts, statistics, and ROI models to satisfy the analytical brain. This is the **Proof Layer**.
