<div><b>Check-In:</b><br></div>
<div>Check-in begins on Thursday, August 19 at 4:00pm, so please leave your luggage in your car until you are able to actually check-in as the front desk reception area does not have sufficient space to store everyone's luggage. You can check-into your room right before or during dinner (6pm to 7:45pm) by visiting the front desk where your keys will be ready for you.  The parking lots are patrolled and have security, so your luggage will be safe.</div>
<div> </div>
<div><b>Parking:</b><br></div>
<div>The property is large, so parking for each guest will be based on your room location. Temporarily, when you arrive, please park under the garage area located immediately as you enter near the front lobby.  There is also beach parking access on both sides of the front entrance that should have ample parking.  The area is patrolled so you can leave your luggage in your car until you check-in later. When you check-in, the front desk will provide you with a map to guide you to the appropriate parking location.  If you experience any difficulty, please call the hotel: 866-867-0976</div>
<div><br></div>

<div>We do <b>not </b>encourage you to bring guests during your EMBA 900A experience as the point of hosting the course off-site is to fully unplug and immerse in the experience.  However, if your personal circumstances do require that you bring a guest, please call the hotel at least 3 days prior to your check-in date to add the guest's name to the account (this is hotel policy). </div>
