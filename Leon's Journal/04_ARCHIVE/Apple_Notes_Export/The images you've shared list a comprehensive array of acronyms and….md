<div>1. **Security Threats and Attacks**:</div>
<div>   - **APT (Advanced Persistent Threat)**: This refers to a prolonged and targeted cyberattack in which an intruder gains access to a network and remains undetected for an extended period of time.</div>
<div>   - **DDoS (Distributed Denial of Service)**: An attack that disrupts the normal traffic of a targeted server, service, or network by overwhelming the target or its surrounding infrastructure with a flood of Internet traffic.</div>

<div>2. **Defense Mechanisms**:</div>
<div>   - **WAF (Web Application Firewall)**: Protects web applications by filtering and monitoring HTTP traffic between a web application and the Internet.</div>
<div>   - **IDS (Intrusion Detection System)** and **IPS (Intrusion Prevention System)**: Systems designed to detect and potentially prevent malicious activities and violations of policies.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>3. **Protocols and Security Measures**:</div>
<div>   - **TLS (Transport Layer Security)**: A protocol that ensures privacy between communicating applications and their users on the Internet.</div>
<div>   - **LDAP (Lightweight Directory Access Protocol)**: Used for accessing and maintaining distributed directory information services over an Internet Protocol (IP) network.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>4. **Access and Identity Management**:</div>
<div>   - **IAM (Identity and Access Management)**: Frameworks and solutions for ensuring that the right individuals have access to the right resources for the right reasons.</div>
<div>   - **MFA (Multi-factor Authentication)**: Requires more than one piece of evidence to authenticate a user, enhancing security by combining two or more independent credentials.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>5. **Compliance and Standards**:</div>
<div>   - **GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)**: Regulation in EU law on data protection and privacy in the European Union and the European Economic Area.</div>
<div>   - **PCI-DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard)**: Security standards for companies that handle branded credit cards to increase controls around cardholder data to reduce credit card fraud.</div>
