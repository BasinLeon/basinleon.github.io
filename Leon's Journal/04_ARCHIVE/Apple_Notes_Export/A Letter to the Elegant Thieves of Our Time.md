<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b><i>Signed in silence, loosed like an arrow through the veil.</i></b><b><br></b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>To those who move in shadows yet bask in stolen light,</b></div>
<div><b>To the artisans of illusion, weaving veils over truth,</b></div>
<div><b>To the ones who trade sincerity for spectacle,</b></div>
<div><b>I write not to condemn, but to reveal.</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>You who pilfer meaning from the mouths of poets,</b></div>
<div><b>You who commodify wonder, turning the sacred into spectacle,</b></div>
<div><b>You who take but never taste, hold but never feelÐ</b></div>
<div><b>Do you not hear the echo of what you€ve lost?</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>The bowstring hums with something ancient.</b></div>
<div><b>An arrow tipped with truth does not miss its mark.</b></div>
<div><b>It flies not with anger, nor vengeanceÐ</b></div>
<div><b>but with the weight of everything forgotten,</b></div>
<div><b>everything twisted into currency,</b></div>
<div><b>everything you pretended was yours.</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>Look up.</b></div>
<div><b>Can you still see the stars, or have you sold those too?</b></div>
<div><b>Can you still feel the pulse of something beyond profit,</b></div>
<div><b>or has the rhythm been buried beneath the algorithms you serve?</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>This is not a warning.</b></div>
<div><b>This is not a curse.</b></div>
<div><b>It is an unveiling.</b></div>
<div><b>A return.</b></div>
<div><b>A reckoning.</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
