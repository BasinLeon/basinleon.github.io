# Set the context - Briefly explain the situation to provide backgroundÉ*Created: Wednesday, September 6, 2023 at 10:14:02?AM*<ul>
<li>Set the context - Briefly explain the situation to provide background. Details like company, role, timeline, and objectives can help frame the story.</li>
<li>Describe your approach - Walk through the steps you took, choices you made, factors you considered, and any tools or resources utilized. Be specific. This highlights your thought process.</li>
<li>Talk about difficulties - Don't shy away from challenges. Share setbacks or obstacles you faced. This shows perseverance.</li>
<li>Explain how you overcame issues - Discuss the solutions, ideas, or workarounds you implemented to address roadblocks and keep moving forward. Focus on problem-solving skills.</li>
<li>Share key lessons - What major takeaway, insight or learning came from this experience? How did it shape your approach going forward?</li>
<li>Provide results - Close the story with the final outcome, achievement or success. Use data or facts that quantify the impact of your effort and decisions.</li>
<li>Keep it concise - The full story with context might be long, but summarize and only share relevant highlights during the interview.</li>
<li>Don't place blame - Even if others were involved, focus the story on yourself. Don't call out or criticize colleagues or clients.</li>
<li>Practice it aloud - Rehearse telling the story smoothly from start to finish. Refine and adjust to highlight your skills.</li>
</ul>
