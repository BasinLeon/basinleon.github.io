# Eternal Sonata: A Birthday Ode to My Mother*Created: Friday, September 1, 2023 at 8:33:03?PM*<div>Eternal Sonata: A Birthday Ode to My Mother<br></div>
<div><br></div>
<div>My dearest Mother, your birthday is like a sacred hymn,</div>
<div>A celestial piece, on life's grand piano, brimming to the brim.</div>
<div>You've been growing your heart, a masterpiece, from dusk to dawn,</div>
<div>With every note you play in life, a new day is born.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>I've changed your life, an unexpected chord, a harmonious shift,</div>
<div>But you, Mom, gave me lifeča crescendo from the Creator's gift.</div>
<div>The black and white keys that mirror your joys and your tears,</div>
<div>Combine to create music that transcends all earthly years.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Majestic and angelic, the presence of the Lord resounds in you,</div>
<div>Like an enduring, classical opus, timeless in each note, eternal and true.</div>
<div>Now, focus inwardly, dear Mom, for your heart has much to tell,</div>
<div>Let it guide your mind and soul, like a pianist in a spell.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Your expressions, Mom, let them sing like a melody on the keys,</div>
<div>A composition of love, depth, and intricate harmonies.</div>
<div>Happy Birthday, my muse, my Mother, my eternal song,</div>
<div>In the concert hall of my heart, it's to you I truly belong.<br></div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Love you, mom! <br></div>
<div>Your son, Leonid (Leon) Basin</div>
