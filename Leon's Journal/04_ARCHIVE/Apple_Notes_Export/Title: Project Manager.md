<div>Primary Responsibilities:</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>1. **Project Planning and Coordination**:</div>
<div>   - Initiate projects by defining scope, objectives, and forming project teams.</div>
<div>   - Plan and designate project resources, prepare budgets, and ensure the alignment with the company's goals and vision</div>
<div>   - Oversee all phases of projects including initiation, planning, execution, monitoring, and closing [oai_citation:2,What does a Project Manager do? Role &amp Responsibilities](https://www.glassdoor.com/Career/project-manager-career_KO0,15.htm#:~:text=A%20project%20manager%20oversees%20all,can%20work%20in%20many%20industries).</div>
<div>   - Maintain a day-to-day schedule, adapting to changing requirements and ensuring efficient and satisfactory project completion [oai_citation:3,Project Manager Job Description - Purdue University](https://www.purdue.edu/projectmanagementcertification/news/project-manager-job-description-career-outlook/#:~:text=As%20the%20name%20suggests%2C%20project,with%20planning%20the%20project).</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>2. **Team Leadership and Management**:</div>
<div>   - Lead project teams of various sizes, delegate tasks, and foster a collaborative working environment.</div>
<div>   - Provide guidance, support, and mentorship to team members, ensuring they have the necessary resources to succeed.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>3. **Communication and Stakeholder Engagement**:</div>
<div>   - Maintain open communication with stakeholders, keeping them informed on project progress, challenges, and solutions.</div>
<div>   - Submit deliverables, obtain necessary approvals, and ensure projects adhere to quality standards and guidelines [oai_citation:4,Project Manager Job Description - Betterteam](https://www.betterteam.com/project-manager-job-description#:~:text=Learn%20about%20the%20key%20requirements%2C,submit%20deliverables%2C%20and%20communicate%20effectively).</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>4. **Performance Monitoring and Reporting**:</div>
<div>   - Monitor project progress, identify and mitigate risks, and ensure projects are on track with timelines and budgets.</div>
<div>   - Prepare and present comprehensive status reports to stakeholders and senior management.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>5. **Problem-Solving and Decision-Making**:</div>
<div>   - Address and resolve project issues, ensuring projects stay on course and meet organizational objectives.</div>
<div>   - Make informed decisions based on data analysis, project metrics, and stakeholder feedback.</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>6. **Quality Assurance and Compliance**:</div>
<div>   - Ensure projects comply with internal policies, industry standards, and regulatory requirements.</div>
<div>   - Conduct regular quality assessments and implement improvements to optimize project outcomes.</div>
<div><br></div>
