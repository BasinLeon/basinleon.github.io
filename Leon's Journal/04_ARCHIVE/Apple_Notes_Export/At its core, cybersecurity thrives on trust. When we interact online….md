<div>AI and Bias: A Conundrum in Ethical Cybersecurity</div>
<div>As we increasingly rely on AI for cybersecurity, we must confront the issue of bias. AI systems are only as good as the data they're trained on. When this data is skewed, the AI's decision-making can be too, leading to unfair or harmful outcomes.</div>
<div>Mitigating AI bias:</div>
<ul>
<li>Diverse Data Sets: Incorporate varied and representative data in AI training to reduce the risk of biased outcomes.</li>
<li>Transparency in AI Processes: Implement clear explanations of how AI systems make decisions, making it easier to identify and correct biases.</li>
<li>Ongoing Monitoring: Regularly review AI decisions for potential biases and make adjustments as needed.</li>
</ul>
