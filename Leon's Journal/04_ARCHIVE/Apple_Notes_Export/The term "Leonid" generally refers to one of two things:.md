# The term "Leonid" generally refers to one of two things:*Created: Wednesday, January 31, 2024 at 4:19:11?PM*<div>The term &quotLeonid&quot generally refers to one of two things:</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>1. **Leonid Meteor Shower**: This is an annual meteor shower that peaks around mid-November. The meteor shower is associated with the comet Tempel-Tuttle, and the meteors in this shower appear to originate from the constellation Leo, hence the name &quotLeonid.&quot</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>2. **A Male Given Name**: &quotLeonid&quot is also a male given name of Greek origin, meaning &quotlion-like.&quot It is quite common in Slavic countries and in Greece (in the form &quotLeonidas&quot). The name may be associated with characteristics of strength and courage, akin to a lion.</div>
