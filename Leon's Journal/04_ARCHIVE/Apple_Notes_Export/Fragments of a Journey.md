# Fragments of a Journey*Created: Friday, May 24, 2024 at 6:46:41?PM*<div>Fragments of a Journey</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Mental fortitude tested.</div>
<div>Health frail, loved ones lost.</div>
<div>Self-doubt whispers, anxiety claws.</div>
<div>A life built on ancient soil,</div>
<div>Now navigating the modern maze.</div>
<div>Cyber guardian by day,</div>
<div>Peeling back the digital skin.</div>
<div>Not to foresee, but to understand.</div>
<div>Lessons from the past,</div>
<div>Guiding lights for the future.</div>
<div>Greatness within, yet dormant.</div>
<div>This journal, a spark, a flame.</div>
<div>To embrace the path,</div>
<div>To unravel the threads of my being.</div>
<div>Words flow, understanding deepens.</div>
<div>A poet's heart, a writer's soul.</div>
<div>Nine books birthed,</div>
<div>Countless stories yet untold.</div>
<div>Website echoes, visitors fade.</div>
<div>Winds change, whispers linger.</div>
<div>Bukowski's spirit,</div>
<div>A kindred soul in the literary night.</div>
<div>Modern verses feel sterile,</div>
<div>Unworthy of the editor's touch.</div>
<div>Yet, this entry blooms,</div>
<div>A poem of identity and becoming.</div>
