<div>The world of sales is filled with ups and downs. Rejections, lost deals, and high-pressure situations are all part of the journey. Emotional regulation, another key aspect of EI, helps salespeople manage their reactions to these situations. It's about maintaining a balanced outlook, not getting too disheartened by the lows or overly excited by the highs. This emotional balance aids in making rational decisions, handling stress, and maintaining a consistent performance.</div>
