# Echoes of the Ivory Path: A Writer's Soliloquy,*Created: Tuesday, December 26, 2023 at 12:06:12?AM*<div>Echoes of the Ivory Path: A Writer's Soliloquy,</div>
<div><br></div>
<div>Beneath the boughs of the family tree,</div>
<div>A writer carves out destiny.</div>
<div>Keys of ivory, dance and flee,</div>
<div>To the tranquil tunes of Debussy.</div>
<div>A single line threads through the heart,</div>
<div>Poetry in life, a family's part.</div>
<div>An apple's legacy, not far to dart,</div>
<div>In the circle's half, begins the art.</div>
<div>A world to mark, a poet's sphere,</div>
<div>Mastering digital keys so clear.</div>
<div>A solo peace, a mind's frontier,</div>
<div>Belief in self, the path sincere.</div>
<div>Yet, if whispers fade in silent plea,</div>
<div>What worth the words of you and me?</div>
<div>To mom alone, do these echoes be,</div>
<div>Or to the world, a soul's decree?</div>
<div>A New Year's promise, a Christmas spark,</div>
<div>In the silent night, we leave our mark.</div>
<div>A light within, against the dark,</div>
<div>Understanding self ą that vital arc.</div>
<div>In the silence, if none do hear,</div>
<div>Does it dim the worth of a writer's tear?</div>
<div>No, for every note, every fear,</div>
<div>Is the might of self, forever near.</div>
<div>So let the music swell and crest,</div>
<div>In the quiet, we find our quest.</div>
<div>To write, to play, to do our best,</div>
<div>In the end, it's our own test.</div>
<div>A story-poem, a New Year's cheer,</div>
<div>With a Christmas glow, we hold dear.</div>
<div>To understand oneself is to steer,</div>
<div>Through life's grand stage, year to year.</div>
