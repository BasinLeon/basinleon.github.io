# My prayers*Created: Thursday, January 1, 2026 at 3:27:08?PM*<div>My prayers </div>
<div><br></div>
<div><b>Leon, I will give you usable prayers, not abstractions. These are grounded, traditional in spirit, and practical for where you actually are right now. Think of them as tools you can return to daily, not poetry for display.</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>Below are five core prayers, each with a purpose. You can read them as written or adapt the language. Consistency matters more than perfection.</b></div>
<div><br></div>
<div>?</div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>1. Morning Grounding Prayer</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>Purpose: Clarity, steadiness, orientation for the day</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>God of truth and breath,</b></div>
<div><b>Thank You for returning my soul today.</b></div>
<div><b>Give me clarity of mind, steadiness of heart,</b></div>
<div><b>and discipline in my actions.</b></div>
<div><b>Help me do the next right thing,</b></div>
<div><b>not everything at once.</b></div>
<div><b>Let my work today serve life,</b></div>
<div><b>protect my family,</b></div>
<div><b>and honor what You placed in me.</b></div>
<div><b>Amen.</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>Use this before email, phone, or news. It sets the tone before the world starts pulling.</b></div>
<div><br></div>
<div>?</div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>2. Prayer for Provision and Work</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>Purpose: Financial pressure, job uncertainty, dignity in effort</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>God who sustains all things,</b></div>
<div><b>You see my effort and my worry.</b></div>
<div><b>Open the right doors,</b></div>
<div><b>close the wrong ones quickly,</b></div>
<div><b>and give me the strength to keep showing up.</b></div>
<div><b>Let my work be honest,</b></div>
<div><b>my income sufficient,</b></div>
<div><b>and my household protected.</b></div>
<div><b>I trust You with the outcome,</b></div>
<div><b>and I commit to the work in front of me.</b></div>
<div><b>Amen.</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>This is not passive. It aligns effort with trust.</b></div>
<div><br></div>
<div>?</div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>3. Prayer for Fatherhood and Family</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>Purpose: Patience, presence, legacy</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>God of generations,</b></div>
<div><b>Help me be calm when I am tired,</b></div>
<div><b>gentle when I am frustrated,</b></div>
<div><b>and present when I want to escape.</b></div>
<div><b>Let my children feel safe in my voice</b></div>
<div><b>and secure in my love.</b></div>
<div><b>Teach me to lead without fear</b></div>
<div><b>and correct without anger.</b></div>
<div><b>May my home be a place of peace.</b></div>
<div><b>Amen.</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>Say this after a hard parenting moment or before bedtime.</b></div>
<div><br></div>
<div>?</div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>4. Short Prayer for Anxiety or Overwhelm</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>Purpose: Nervous system reset, emotional regulation</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>God, I am overwhelmed.</b></div>
<div><b>Slow my breath.</b></div>
<div><b>Quiet my thoughts.</b></div>
<div><b>Bring me back to what is real</b></div>
<div><b>and what is within my control.</b></div>
<div><b>I place this weight down now.</b></div>
<div><b>Amen.</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>This works best spoken out loud. Simple and effective.</b></div>
<div><br></div>
<div>?</div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>5. Night Reflection Prayer</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>Purpose: Release, integration, sleep</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>Thank You for what I handled well today.</b></div>
<div><b>Forgive what I mishandled.</b></div>
<div><b>Take what is unfinished and hold it for me.</b></div>
<div><b>Guard my sleep, restore my strength,</b></div>
<div><b>and prepare me for tomorrow.</b></div>
<div><b>I rest now.</b></div>
<div><b>Amen.</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>Say this even if the day felt like a mess. Especially then.</b></div>
<div><br></div>
