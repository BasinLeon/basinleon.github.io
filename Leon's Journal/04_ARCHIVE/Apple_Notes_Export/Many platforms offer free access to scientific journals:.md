# Many platforms offer free access to scientific journals:*Created: Thursday, July 11, 2024 at 4:07:12?PM*<div>Many platforms offer free access to scientific journals:</div>
<div> * Directory of Open Access Journals (DOAJ): Curated directory of high-quality, peer-reviewed journals.</div>
<div> * CORE: Aggregates millions of open-access articles.</div>
<div> * ScienceOpen: Research network with free access after registration.</div>
<div> * Public Library of Science (PLOS): Non-profit publisher of open-access journals.</div>
<div> * Unpaywall: Browser extension that finds free versions of paywalled articles.</div>
<div>These are just a few options. Consider your specific research needs when choosing a platform.</div>
