<div>In the heart of age-old Egypt, where obelisks pierce the heavens and the mighty pharaoh, an emblem of terrestrial dominion, presides in unwavering might. His power, rooted in tumult and conflict, strikes at the very essence of one's being, causing wounds that reverberate deep within the soul.</div>
