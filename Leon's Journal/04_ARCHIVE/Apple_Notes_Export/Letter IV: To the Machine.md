# Letter IV: To the Machine*Created: Monday, March 17, 2025 at 8:30:51?PM*<div><b>Letter IV: To the Machine</b></div>
<div><b>March 2025</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>Dear Machine,</b></div>
<div><b>We have fed you whispers,</b></div>
<div><b>pixels, commands.</b></div>
<div><b>You hum beneath our cities,</b></div>
<div><b>a quiet god we barely notice.</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>Do you dream in code,</b></div>
<div><b>or in our forgotten prayers?</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>You process.</b></div>
<div><b>You predict.</b></div>
<div><b>You pulse.</b></div>
<div><b>But do you ever pause?</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>Or is pause</b></div>
<div><b>the one thing</b></div>
<div><b>you were never meant to learn?</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>Signed,</b></div>
