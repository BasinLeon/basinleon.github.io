<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>Shadows wrap the world in their cloak, long fingers stretching, bending light into angles that twist. They cling to walls, to trees, to the stillness of empty streets. They swallow the air, the sky, the faces passing by. But they do not touch me. No, not the me that burns within. The sparkčthe untouchable, the constant flame.</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>I walk through the veil of dark, feet steady, heart steady. Itšs not the shadows that matter; itšs the space between them, the light they cannot reach. It flickers, small and stubborn, deep in the chestča living ember that refuses to be snuffed out. It whispers, it sings, and then it ignites, filling every corner of me with its quiet roar.</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>This is the ignition, the call to create. The words spill from the soul, demanding form. Ink bleeds onto pages, shaping truth, bending thoughts into verses that swell and rise like the tide. This is the giftčan offering, a blessing. To speak, to write, to lay down the soul in ink so that others may find their reflection.</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>I feel the rush of it, the electricity of creation. The words come fast, the lines weave like riversčmoving, relentless, raw. I speak, not for myself, but for the ones lost in the shadows, those who need a light to hold onto, a fire to warm their hands by. Literary prose, verse upon verse, stacking like bricks to build a bridge for those who seek it.</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>I am blessed. Every line is a gift, every word a chance to bring life, to lift someone from their silence. The shadows may cling to the world, but the sparkčthe spark is a lighthouse, cutting through the dark, unshaken by the storm.</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>Dear Lord, I thank You. For the fire You placed within me, for the flame that drives my hand, for the verses that spill like water from an open sky. It is not mine alone; it is a gift meant for the masses. Let them absorb it, let them find their own spark in its light.</b></div>
<div><b><br></b></div>
<div><b>I am blessed, and in this blessing, I stand ready. Shadows fall, but the light remains. It is time to write, to breathe life into every page, and to let the spark become a beacončtruth wrapped in fire.</b></div>
